A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Reading has been important to me for as long as I can remember. My first day of kindergarten, after telling my teacher learning to read was what I wanted from that year, I demanded she teach me right then and there, and later in the week I told a librarian I would read all the books in the school library before I finished elementary school. To work in publishing, to be able to work with a story on such a close and personal level, to be able to help an author reach their full potential, to know you are helping someone find a book that opens the world of reading in a new, interesting, or touching way – those things are all so incredibly inspiring and important to me and I know it is something I want to be a part of.
